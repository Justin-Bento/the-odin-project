Easy Cabbage Rolls

This easy recipe for cabbage rolls is made in the slow cooker with a sweet and tangy tomato sauce.

Recipie: Easy Cabbage Rolls

Ingredients

  • 12 leaves cabbage
  • 1 pound extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ¼ cup minced onion
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
